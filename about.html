About AgriWaste to Worth

Pioneering sustainable agriculture through AI-powered waste management

Our Mission

AgriWaste to Worth is an AI-powered platform revolutionizing agricultural waste management by connecting farmers, buyers, and processors in a seamless ecosystem. We transform agricultural residues into valuable resources, creating economic opportunities while promoting environmental sustainability.

Key Technologies

  • Deep Learning for waste classification (CNN models)
  • Machine Learning for price prediction (Regression & Time-series)
  • Computer Vision for quality assessment
  • Recommendation engines for buyer-seller matching
  • Big Data analytics for market insights
